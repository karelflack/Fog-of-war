How to Play
Objective

Race the AI to complete a nuclear weapon. You need three things: 100% science, 100% depleted uranium, and a rocket silo placed on the map. When all three are ready, initiate assembly — a 4-minute countdown. On completion your ICBM launches toward the enemy capital. First to launch wins.

Structures — left sidebar
  • Lab (500c) — Generates science over time. Place several across different regions. Can be sabotaged and temporarily disabled.
  • Reactor (700c) — Generates depleted uranium. Build multiple — uranium takes time to fill. Can be found and permanently destroyed.
  • Rocket Silo (1200c) — Required to launch. Only one can be built. A prime sabotage target — hide it or protect it with jammers.
  • Jammer (600c) — Dual-purpose: near your labs it penalises enemy operations (−28% success). Near enemy labs it slows their science by 50%. Enemy jammers are hidden until swept or reconed.
Operations — right sidebar
  • RECON (300c · 45s) — Scans a radius around your click. Reveals enemy labs, jammers, reactors, and silos in range.
  • STEAL (500c · 70s) — Targets a known or estimated enemy region. On success, transfers a portion of their science to you.
  • SABOTAGE (700c · 100s) — Click near a revealed enemy structure. Permanently destroys jammers, reactors, and silos. Disables labs for 2 minutes.
Counter-Intel
  • SWEEP (1500c) — All your labs simultaneously scan their surrounding area. Reveals hidden enemy jammers, reactors, and silos so you can target them with SABOTAGE.
  • Your own jammers are always visible. Enemy jammers must be located first via RECON or SWEEP before you can destroy them.
Tips
  • Build 2–3 reactors early — uranium takes a long time to fill from zero.
  • Place your silo in a remote region to delay enemy detection.
  • Surround your labs and silo with jammers to hamper enemy operations.
  • If you find the enemy silo — SABOTAGE it immediately. Destroying it while they are assembling interrupts the countdown.
  • Watch the Intel panel (right sidebar) for revealed structures — it shows coordinates for SABOTAGE targeting.
  • On Hard difficulty the AI moves fast. Sweep early and often.
